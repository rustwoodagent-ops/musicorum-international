# Musicorum International Website

A polished, responsive one-page business website for Musicorum International — music coaching and live performances in Scarborough, Queensland, Australia.

## About

Musicorum International is a combined business hub for:
- Music coaching for children, beginners and adults
- Live music performances for cafés, events, showcases and special occasions

## Features

- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first, works on all devices
- **Fast Loading**: Lightweight static site
- **Accessible**: Clean semantic HTML, good contrast
- **SEO Ready**: Proper meta tags and semantic structure
- **Smooth Navigation**: Sticky header with smooth scrolling
- **Contact Integration**: Clickable phone and email links

## Sections

1. **Hero** — Full-screen intro with main portrait
2. **About** — Business story and mission
3. **Coaching** — Music lessons and services
4. **Performances** — Live music for events
5. **Showcases** — Student experiences and events
6. **Gallery** — Visual showcase of work
7. **Contact** — Phone, email and location
8. **Footer** — Social links and copyright
