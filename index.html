About Musicorum International

Musicorum International is built around a love of music, learning and live performance. Darren works with children, beginners and adults, offering supportive coaching that helps students build skill, confidence and enjoyment.

Alongside coaching, Musicorum International also brings live music into cafés, showcases and events, creating memorable musical experiences for audiences and students alike.

From first lessons to public performance, Musicorum International is about helping music feel real, rewarding and accessible.
